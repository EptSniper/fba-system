### Beginner tactics worth knowing early (from the course videos)
- **Start FBM, not FBA** — faster learning + payouts, no storage/inbound fees; go FBA once fluent.
- **Grocery is the easiest first category** — cheap, easy to ship, and **non-returnable** (profit is locked once sold).
- **Break even first** on auto-ungated brands to build sales history → Amazon trust → easier ungating later.
- **Reverse sourcing** (copy what other small sellers sell) is the fastest way to find products — see [`../playbooks/sourcing-playbook.md`](../playbooks/sourcing-playbook.md).
