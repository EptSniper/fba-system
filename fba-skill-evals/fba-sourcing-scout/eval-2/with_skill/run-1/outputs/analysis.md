## Instant-reject / red-flag screen — apply BEFORE adding anything to cart

Kill a lead on the spot if any of these are true (don't waste the analyst's time):

- **LEGO/Pokemon offer brawl** — if new-offer count is climbing toward the cap or already in a pile-in (>1.4× its 90-day average), the price is about to tank. BOGO toys are *the* thing every reseller grabs, so this is the #1 killer this week.
- **Amazon holds the Buy Box** (now, or ≥20% historically) → hard reject. Toys are a category Amazon plays in directly.
- **Price spike** — current Amazon price >1.5× its 90-day average → will revert before your FBA units land (~2-week delay).
- **IP cliff** — offer count crashed and never recovered (e.g. 56→1) → brand filed IP complaints. Worse than a price drop.
- **Disney-licensed / avoid-brand** toy → skip unless you've already confirmed eligibility.
- **Too easy to find** — Target-exclusive or private-label line with no real Amazon listing, or the deal is so obvious every seller has it → no moat.
- **Out of the $8–$60 sell band** → off-strategy for a beginner; cheap toys especially often can't clear profit ≥ $3 after fees.
- **No featured offer / "see all buying options" only** → far slower sales.

## Sizing the buy (when a lead survives the gates)

- Beginner rule: **5–10 units per product** until you see how it actually moves. Do NOT load up on BOGO volume just because it's cheap.
- Formula: `variation monthly sales ÷ (price-competitive sellers + you)`, then **cut 30–50%**.
- Worst-case check: only buy if, at the **lowest historical Buy-Box price**, you break even or lose ≤ $1–2/unit.
- Anti-flag when ordering: keep the first order small, pay with PayPal/Apple/Google Pay, and if Target flags you for reselling, message customer service. **List the ASIN in Seller Central BEFORE you buy — if it's hard-gated, don't buy it.**

## Verdict & next steps

**Worth building a run around — yes.** A toy BOGO + Target Circle is a textbook deal-first, stackable event, and toy aisles are dense with friendly/ungated brands. The catch specific to BOGO toys: *everyone* sources them, so offer pile-in and Amazon-in-the-Buy-Box are your two biggest reject reasons. Win by stacking deeper (Circle + discounted GC + best cashback) and by being disciplined on the red-flag screen rather than buying volume because it's cheap.
