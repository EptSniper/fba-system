## Content (condensed)

The SAS Charts panel is powered by licensed Keepa data, built into every SAS lookup. A product's
current price and rank tell you almost nothing on their own; the Charts panel shows how a listing
has actually behaved over time so you judge demand and price stability instead of guessing from a
single snapshot.

What it shows:
- Historic sales rank (BSR), Amazon price, Buy Box price, lowest FBA and lowest FBM prices, plus
  offer and review counts.
- Switchable 30 / 90 / 180-day or full-history views; zoom and pan to read exact prices and counts
  at any point in time.

How SellerAmp says to read it for decisions:
- Frequent BSR drops indicate regular sales; use rank history to sanity-check estimated monthly sales.
- A stable price suggests less risk of a race to the bottom; use price history to spot whether the
  current price is normal or a temporary spike.
- Together, rank + price history tell you whether a profitable-looking deal is also a *reliable* one.
