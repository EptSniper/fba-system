### How leads flow
1. **Source** → a lead lands here (my find, mentor tip, or scout Discord alert).
2. **Research** → fill the [buy/no-buy template](../ai-system/product-research-template.md); update the row's status.
3. **Decide** → ✅ BUY / ⏸️ review / ❌ pass.
4. **Order & track** → move units into [`inventory.md`](inventory.md), costs into [`finances.md`](finances.md).
5. **Outcome** → label good/bad so the `scout` AI learns (see ai-system vision).
