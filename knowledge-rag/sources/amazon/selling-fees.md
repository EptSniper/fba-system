# Amazon standard selling fees

Two basic types: **selling-plan fees** and **referral fees**. FBA fulfillment/storage are separate
optional costs (see fba-overview.md). Confirm the exact per-SKU fee in the Revenue Calculator.

## Selling plans
- **Individual:** $0.99 per item sold. Lists one product at a time, static prices, FBA or FBM.
- **Professional:** $39.99 / month. Adds bulk listing, Automate Pricing, Brand Registry tools,
  Amazon Ads, B2B, Global Selling, apps/APIs, and the New Seller Guide. Worth it above ~40 items/mo.

**Why this matters for arbitrage:** referral fee is category-specific, so the "15% default" the
scout assumes is only a starting point — Electronics/Computers (8%) are friendlier, Device
Accessories (45%) brutal. Plug the real category fee into the buy/no-buy math before sourcing.
