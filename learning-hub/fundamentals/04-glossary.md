# 04 · Glossary — Amazon Seller Terms

*The vocabulary, in plain English. Skim it once; come back when a term shows up.*

### Core marketplace
- **Seller Central** — your seller dashboard (`sell.amazon.com`): listings, orders, payments, reports.
- **ASIN** — Amazon's 10-char product ID (e.g. `B0XXXXXXXX`). One per product page.
- **SKU** — *your* internal code for a unit you sell.
- **FNSKU** — the Amazon barcode that ties a physical unit in the warehouse to *your* inventory.
- **UPC / EAN** — manufacturer barcodes used to match/create listings.
- **Listing** — the product page. Many sellers can sell on the *same* listing.
- **Buy Box / Featured Offer** — the highlighted "Add to Cart" offer. ~80%+ of sales flow through it. Requires a Professional account + good price/health/stock.

### Fulfillment
- **FBA** (Fulfilled by Amazon) — you ship inventory to Amazon; they store, pick, pack, ship, and handle returns. Gets the Prime badge.
- **FBM** (Fulfilled by Merchant) — you store and ship yourself.
- **SFP** (Seller-Fulfilled Prime) — you ship yourself but still earn the Prime badge (strict standards).
- **Prep / Prep center / 3PL** — preparing units to Amazon's standards (labels, poly-bags, bundling). A 3PL/prep center can receive your OA orders and prep+forward them to FBA for you.
- **Inbound shipment** — the box of inventory you send into Amazon's network.

### Money & fees
- **Referral fee** — Amazon's commission per sale, **~15%** for most categories (range 8–15%, some higher), $0.30 minimum.
- **FBA fulfillment fee** — per-unit pick/pack/ship charge based on size & weight tier.
- **Storage fee** — monthly charge per cubic foot; **higher in Oct–Dec**.
- **Aged / long-term storage fee** — surcharge on inventory sitting **181+ days**.
- **Low-inventory-level fee** — penalty on fast-moving SKUs you let run too low.
- **Inbound placement fee** — charge when Amazon splits your shipment across warehouses (largely avoidable).
- **Fuel & logistics surcharge** — **3.5%** on fulfillment fees (added April 2026).
- **COGS** — Cost Of Goods Sold (what you paid for the item + getting it to Amazon).
- **ROI** — Return On Investment = profit ÷ COGS. OA sellers often target **≥30%**.
- **Margin (net)** — profit ÷ sale price, *after all fees*. Target ~**25–35%** for PL; OA varies.
- **Disbursement** — Amazon paying your balance to your bank (~every 2 weeks).

### Research tools
- **Keepa** — price, sales-rank (BSR), Buy Box, and offer history for any product. The industry-standard history layer. (~$20/mo)
- **SellerAmp SAS** — sourcing/analysis tool + profit calculator (Chrome extension + app). Instantly shows ROI, profit, BSR, eligibility, and Keepa data while you browse. Built for OA/RA/wholesale. (~$20/mo)
- **Amazon Revenue Calculator** — Amazon's own fee/profit estimator. The final check before buying.
- **BSR (Best Sellers Rank)** — a category rank that proxies how fast something sells (lower = sells faster). Use as a *filter*, confirm with Keepa history.
- **Sales Rank Drops** — how often the rank dropped (≈ a sale happened); a sales-velocity estimate.

### Restrictions & risk
- **Gated / Restricted category or brand** — products you must get *approved* to sell.
- **Ungating** — getting that approval, usually via **wholesale invoices** (10+ units, authorized distributor, recent date) — *retail receipts no longer work*.
- **IP complaint** — a brand alleging you're not authorized to sell theirs; can suspend a listing. A core OA risk.
- **Brand Registry** — Amazon program for brand *owners* (relevant once doing private label).
- **Hazmat** — items with batteries/flammable/pressurized contents; extra review and rules.

### Sourcing & deals
- **Manual sourcing** — browsing retailer clearance/sale pages in *real time* for deals (catches live prices; the course author's preferred method).
- **Reverse sourcing / storefront stalking** — opening competitors' storefronts to see which brands/products they sell, then sourcing those.
- **BOLO** — "Be On the Lookout": a hot/trending item worth grabbing.
- **Cashback & coupon tools** — Rakuten, TopCashback, Coupert (coupons); **RevROI** compares cashback rates. Used to lower COGS so thin deals clear margin.
- **Gift-card / coupon stacking** — combining discounts (sale + coupon + cashback + discounted gift cards) to hit a target margin.
- **Keepa Product Finder** — Keepa's Data → Product Finder; filter by brand + sales rank + Amazon-out-of-stock + min offers to surface the top listings of a brand fast.
- **Auto-ungate** — a brand you can sell with no invoice (focus here first). **Hard-gate** — a brand you can't even apply to sell (e.g. Nike). See [ungating playbook](../playbooks/ungating-playbook.md).
- **Instant denial** — an ungating application bot-rejected in <5–10 min; needs a 24–48h cooldown or a different ASIN.
- **Landed cost** — true cost of a unit *including shipping* (a $10 item + $9.95 shipping = $20). Always use landed cost in the calculator.
- **Base hits** — products that each make ~$100–200/mo; the bulk of OA income (vs rare "home run" products).
- **Yellow sold-line** — Keepa's "X sold/mo" marker; if absent, the item sells <50/mo (usually pass).

### Operations & setup
- **Boxem** — all-in-one ungating/listing/shipping tool with a **bulk auto-ungate checker** (boxem.com).
- **Manufacturing margin** — making a thin deal profitable by *stacking* discounts (coupons, cashback, gift cards, rewards).
- **Tax-exempt / reseller certificate** — buy sales-tax-free (B2B) at Home Depot/Lowe's/Walmart; same thing, named differently per state.
- **EIN** — free IRS "SSN for your business"; **LLC** = legal protection; **DBA** = business banking, no protection.
- **FNSKU label (2×1)** — Amazon unit barcode, FBA only; **4×6 label** — shipping/box label (FBM + FBA boxes).
- **Do-not-separate sticker** — tells the warehouse not to split a multipack.
- **Liquidation / removal** — Amazon programs to clear or return unsold FBA stock.

### Models
- **OA / RA** — Online / Retail Arbitrage (buy low elsewhere, resell on Amazon).
- **Wholesale** — bulk buying from brands/distributors with invoices.
- **Private Label (PL)** — your own brand/product on your own listing.

### Growth (later)
- **PPC / Sponsored Products** — pay-per-click ads on Amazon.
- **ACoS / TACoS** — ad spend ÷ ad sales (ACoS) / ÷ total sales (TACoS); ad-efficiency metrics.
- **A+ Content** — enhanced images/text on a listing (brand owners).
- **Variations / Parent-child ASIN** — sizes/colors grouped under one parent listing.
- **Subscribe & Save** — recurring-purchase program (builds repeat revenue).
- **Vine** — Amazon program to seed early reviews.
- **MOQ** — Minimum Order Quantity a supplier requires.
