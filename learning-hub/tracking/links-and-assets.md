### How this works
- When Mehmet shares a **screenshot/file**, Claude saves it to `../assets/` (named
  `YYYY-MM-DD_topic.ext`) and adds a row here.
- When Mehmet shares a **link**, Claude adds a row (and fetches/summarizes it if useful).
- Anything that becomes a lesson also gets filed into `fundamentals/` or `transcripts/insights.md`.

*Note: chat-pasted images aren't always saved to disk as files automatically. When
that happens, Claude stores a faithful **text transcription** here (so the AI can
still use the content) and notes that the original lives in the chat.*
