## The 5 headline Keepa Product Finder (KPF) power moves
1. **Stalk multiple storefronts at once** — batch several seller IDs into one KPF search instead of paging
   sellers one at a time.
2. **Use KPF to find profitable FBM inventory** — heavier/oversized items FBA sellers tend to skip.
3. **Set KPF filters for stable-priced ASINs** — low price variance = less race-to-the-bottom risk.
4. **Use KPF to find brand-new products (<1 month old)** — get in before offer count saturates.
5. **Reverse-source by brand with KPF** — expand from one proven brand to its full catalog.

## Advanced KPF tactics named in the SOP list (worth knowing by name)
- "Lead synthesizer" — turn one good lead into many new leads.
- A2A (Amazon-to-Amazon) flips — for pricing mistakes, holiday/seasonal flips, future products.
- KPF **negative keywords**; filtering by seller ID; bookmarking/exporting KPF searches to monitor.
- "Buy the pinch" (out-of-stock-driven) sourcing; tracking ASINs that frequently fall OOS.
- Finding ASINs with **no current sales rank** via BSR history + review-count growth.
- Estimating monthly sales from review count; sub-rank viewing; "up-down chart" verification.
- Cross-border arbitrage via Keepa's Compare International Prices feature; bundle sourcing.
