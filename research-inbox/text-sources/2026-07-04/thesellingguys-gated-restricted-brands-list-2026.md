## Distilled content

**Scale:** 750+ brands tracked in one alphabetical table, tiered by risk status:
1. Legal action or policy warning (most severe)
2. Gated (Amazon-restricted)
3. Cease-and-desist email from brand owner
4. IP-infringement warning
5. Issues/restrictions reported — some risk
6. Some restrictions — no legal action reported (lower risk)

**Notable fully gated (Amazon-enforced):** Nike, Lego (US only), Asics, Fitbit, Britax, Logitech, Under Armour, Sennheiser, National Geographic, Tums.

**Notable legal action / C&D (avoid):** Apple, Bose, Canon (cameras), Braun, Ninja, NutriBullet, Magic Bullet, Razer, Razor, Skullcandy, Jabra, Crosley, Makita, Netgear, Seagate, Polk, Johnson & Johnson, KONG, SmartWool, Tatcha, Lime Crime, Audio-Technica, Beachbody.

**Notable IP-warning tier:** SodaStream, Vital Proteins, Supergoop, Neato Robotics, Eero, Goop, Renew Life, USAopoly, ULAK, TORRAS.

**Key guidance (quotable):**
- Gating varies by country (Lego: gated US, sellable UK at time of writing) — but "if a brand owner has taken action in one country, it shows they will take action," so they avoid listing those brands anywhere.
- Author's stance: "not worth the risk of selling any of the brands listed, particularly if you are a new seller."
- Inventory trap: sellers get caught holding stock when the brand owner turns to their country — can escalate to policy warning or suspension; don't stock up on risky brands.
- Checking: BuyBotPro's IP Radar / eligibility checker / auto-ungating cited (vendor plug); the durable rule is check eligibility per-ASIN on YOUR account before buying.
