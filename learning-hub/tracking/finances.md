### Rules I follow
- **Every** buy and payout gets a row — no exceptions.
- Track **net after fees**, not gross. Gross numbers lie.
- A product isn't "profitable" until it's **sold and paid out**, minus returns.
- Keep ~**$500–$2,000** as my working inventory budget while learning.
