### Rules I follow
- Log units the moment I **order**, update when they reach **FBA**.
- Watch the **181-day** aged-inventory line and **Q4** storage rates — clear slow movers before they cost me.
- Restock fast movers at ~**45–60 days** of supply to dodge the low-inventory fee and lost Buy Box.
- Flag anything **stranded** (live but unsellable) immediately.
