# Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)

## What FBA is
Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) lets a seller outsource order fulfillment to Amazon and offer
customers free two-day Prime shipping. You send products into Amazon's fulfillment-center
network; Amazon picks, packs, ships, and handles customer service and returns. FBA is part of
Amazon Supply Chain Services (ASCS). A Professional selling account is $39.99/month + selling fees.

## How FBA works (the 6 steps)
1. **Register for FBA** — in Seller Central: gear icon → Account info → Manage → Register for FBA.
2. **List and assign products to FBA.** Before selecting products:
   - Confirm eligibility via **FBA product restrictions** (Seller Central help G200140860).
   - Review **FBA policies and requirements** (G201030350).
   - Estimate costs in the **Revenue Calculator** (sellercentral.amazon.com/revcalpublic).
   Assign during listing by choosing "Amazon will ship and provide customer service (FBA)".
3. **Send shipment to Amazon** via the **Send to Amazon** workflow — it guides prep (G201021850),
   packing (G200141500), and labeling (G200178470).
4. **Amazon handles fulfillment** — receives/stores inventory, packs and ships orders, and handles
   customer service and returns.
5. **Manage FBA inventory** with the FBA Dashboard, FBA Restock tool, Replenishment Alerts, and
   FBA Liquidations to keep balanced inventory and avoid long-term storage costs.
6. **Optimize and expand** with FBA services and programs (Amazon Global Logistics, Remote
   Fulfillment, Amazon Export).

## FBA costs (what eats your margin)
- **Fulfillment costs** — picking, packing, shipping, customer service, returns; based on the
  product's price, weight, and dimensions (size tier).
- **Storage costs** — charged monthly on the cubic-feet space your inventory occupies (daily avg).
- **Aged inventory** — monthly surcharge on items stored more than 181 days; keep inventory healthy.
- **Returns processing** — charged when Amazon provides a customer free return shipping.
- **Removal / disposal / liquidation** — per-item charge to get inventory back or cleared.
- **FBA inbound placement service** — cost to distribute inventory across fulfillment centers
  closer to customers (standard and large-bulky).
- Use the **Revenue Calculator** to compare FBA vs your own fulfillment per SKU before buying.

## Eligibility & restrictions (the "am I allowed?" gate)
Most products are FBA-eligible, but **some require prior approval (gating)** and **certain products
are not eligible or must meet specific requirements**. Always check FBA product restrictions
(G200140860) for the specific ASIN/category before sourcing. New Professional sellers may be
auto-enrolled in the **FBA New Selection** program (free monthly storage, rebates, waivers on
eligible new products).
