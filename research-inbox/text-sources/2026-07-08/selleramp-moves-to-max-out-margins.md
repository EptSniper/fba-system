## Why it matters for this project
Deeper SAS feature knowledge feeds `fba-selleramp-analyst` and gives the operator concrete moves beyond the
quick profit/ROI check. Several features (Notes & Tags, History, Offers Panel) map to the same lead/DB
concepts the project's trackers use.

## Key takeaways (10 features)
1. **Profit Calculator what-ifs** — model "shipping +10%" / "packaging ×2" and compare **FBM vs FBA side by
   side** (the more-profitable path flips seasonally). Include packaging + labor, not just fees.
2. **Custom Settings are the foundation** — wrong sales-tax, prep/inbound, return-rate, labor or packaging
   makes every downstream ROI wrong. Update quarterly for fee/inflation drift.
3. **Offers Panel** — seller name/rating/review-count/fulfilment type, live stock levels (max/min order-qty
   signal), price incl+excl shipping, your ROI if you matched, lowest-10 prices, FBA-vs-FBM seller split,
   Prime-only filter. Use it to spot weak / low-stock / overpriced competition.
4. **Google Sheets export** — push leads to Sheets as a team/VA lead database with approval-status, tags,
   profit-target columns.
5. **History tab** — re-check previously-analysed "almost profitable" leads; shifted price/competition can
   flip an old reject into a buy.
6. **Notes & Tags** — build a personal sourcing DB (tag Seasonal / High-Margin / Brand-Restricted / Heavy;
   notes like "fragile", "fast seller", "avoid – returns issue"); filter/export by tag.
7. **Charts Panel** (Keepa-powered) — hover for exact price/rank/offer points; zoom/pan 30-day ↔ 1-year.
8. **AI Image Search** — photo → correct Amazon listing even with no link; catches mismatched/duplicate
   listings before you buy.
9. **QVS (Quick View Simplified)** — ASIN, is-Amazon-selling, FBA/FBM seller counts, variations, BSR, and
   first-review snapshot right on the Amazon search page (no click-through).
10. **Variation navigation** — check each variant's own rank/margin/price; low-competition variants are
    where profit is commonly left behind.

**Bonus:** keep a 10–15% margin buffer for seasonal volatility; track packaging/sustainability costs via
Tags. **Related:** SAS **Advanced Search** launched Jun 10 2026 — saved/shareable custom filter searches
(sales rank, price history, stock levels), relevant to scout-style saved queries.
