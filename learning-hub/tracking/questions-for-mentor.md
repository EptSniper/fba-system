# Questions for My Mentor

*My dad's friend — online arbitrage since 2017. A live expert is my biggest
advantage. Bring this list to calls; I'll add answers inline and move the gold into
the fundamentals + the buy/no-buy checklist.*

> Tip: ask **how he actually decides**, not just what's possible. His real rules of
> thumb are worth more than any course.

## Getting started
- For a complete beginner, start **Individual or Professional** plan? When did you switch?
- What did your **first month** actually look like — spend, hours, mistakes?
- Biggest thing you wish you'd known on day one?

## Sourcing (the core skill)
- Where do you find deals now — which **stores/sites**, and how do you get alerted?
- Do you use **deal lists / sourcing groups**, or only self-source? Worth the money?
- How many products do you scan to find one buy? What's your **hit rate**?
- How do you decide **how many units** to buy on a first test?

## Tools
- How exactly do you use **SellerAmp SAS** day-to-day? Any settings I should copy?
- What do you read first on a **Keepa** chart? Any red flags you bail on instantly?
- Any other tools you actually pay for (and which you dropped)?

## Gating, IP & restrictions (the OA minefield)
- Which **brands/categories** do you avoid because they file **IP complaints**?
- What's your **ungating** process — which invoices/distributors work?
- Ever had a listing or account **flagged**? How did you handle it?

## Prep & logistics
- Do you **prep yourself** or use a **prep center / 3PL**? Who/what do you recommend?
- How do you handle **shipping into FBA** cheaply and avoid placement fees?

## Money & risk
- What **ROI / margin** do you require after fees before you'll buy?
- How do you manage **cash flow** between buying and getting paid out?
- How do you handle **returns** and stranded/unsold inventory?

## Scaling & the bigger picture
- When did it become worth your time? What does a realistic monthly profit look like?
- Would you start with OA again today, or go straight to **wholesale/private label**?
- Would you be open to looking at a few of my **first leads** before I buy?
