# Amazon FBA New Selection Program (2026) — launches July 30, 2026

Nova Analytics (vendor blog) reporting a Seller Central announcement (June 18, 2026), corroborated
by Ecomcrew (June 20). Primary source: Seller Central forum post t/137ef04f... and
sell.amazon.com/blog/fba-new-selection-program.

## What's changing [policy, per cited Amazon announcement]
- FBA New Selection Program (2026) **launches July 30, 2026**, replacing the existing program.
- **Bigger inbound-placement fee credit.**
- **90 days of free monthly storage on the first 100 units** of a qualifying parent ASIN.
- **Reduced referral fee on the first $25,000 of revenue per new branded ASIN, for the first 365 days.**
- Existing enrollees **migrate automatically**; new SKUs qualify only if they meet the eligibility
  window on/after the launch date. Requires fresh listing data + brand-registry coverage.

## Why it matters [practitioner]
- Mostly a **private-label / brand-owner** lever (needs Brand Registry + new branded ASINs), so it is
  **not directly usable for pure OA** on existing listings — flag it as context, not an OA tactic.
- Relevance to this operation: if the catalog strategy ever adds a private-label/bundle SKU, time the
  launch to land **after July 30** to start the 365-day reduced-referral clock; the storage waiver
  caps at 100 units, so it rewards a clean, undersized first inbound rather than a big hopeful one.
- Nova claims the credit lifts launch-ASIN contribution by ~4–7 points depending on ASP (vendor est.,
  unverified).

## Note
No change to OA buy-gates in ai-brain.json — this is a new-ASIN subsidy, orthogonal to reselling on
existing listings. Recorded for market/fee awareness.
