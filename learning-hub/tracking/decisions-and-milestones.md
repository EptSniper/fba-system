**Decision:** Begin with **online arbitrage**, graduate to wholesale/private label later.

and the tool stack (Keepa + SellerAmp) is OA-shaped. PL stays the upgrade path (the
`01_research_brief.md` + `scout` are built for it).
**Status:** Tentative — **confirm with mentor** before spending.

### 2026-06-19 — Track everything in a full knowledge base
**Decision:** This chat is my **learning hub**; Claude captures every session, link,
and screenshot into structured, AI/website-ready files under `learning-hub/`.
**Why:** I want the knowledge to compound and feed the AI we're building (and a future
control center). Nothing lost.

### 2026-06-19 — The AI's job is decision-support, not autopilot
**Decision:** The AI **finds items, judges buy/no-buy from my research, tracks finances,
and shows inventory** — but I make the final call; it never moves money or auto-buys.
**Why:** Honest, safe scope (see `04_limitations.md`); SellerAmp + Keepa feed it, my
real outcomes train it.
