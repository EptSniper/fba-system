## OA-friendly brands mentioned (resellable, name-brand)
**Toys / collectibles:** Jellycat, Monster Jam, LEGO, Hot Wheels, McFarlane Toys, Barbie, Paw Patrol, Sunny Angels (blind boxes), Pokémon, Sesame Street, Tonie's/Tonies, Miss Rachel.
**Drinkware / lifestyle:** Stanley, Yeti, Owala, Tito's (mugs).
**Apparel / shoes:** Crocs (+ Jibbitz), Puma (ungateable), Gap, Lululemon, Nautica, Carter's, Tommy Hilfiger, Under Armour, New Balance, Hoka, Adidas/Nike/Jordan (often hard-gated).
**Beauty / personal care:** Dove, Mrs. Meyer's, Native, various perfumes/skincare.
**Grocery:** Fancy Feast (pet), molasses/coffee/snack items, **Dill Pickle Hot Cheetos** (a viral example).
**Tools:** Milwaukee, Cricut (accessories), DJI (drone parts).
**Video games:** EA titles, Call of Duty, Nintendo Switch (physical copies).
**Auto-ungate examples:** Stafford, Cuddle Duds, many Walmart house items.

> Pattern: durable consumer brands with steady demand and **no private-label lock**. Avoid brands known
> for **IP complaints** and **hard-gated** giants (Nike, Adidas) as a beginner.

## Sourcing websites mentioned
**Big retailers:** Walmart.com, Target.com (also the best **ungating** source), Belk, Kohl's (Kohl's Cash loop), Michaels, Staples, Office Depot/"Office Supply."
**Brand / specialty sites:** Crocs.com, Gap, Lululemon, Sephora, Ulta, Nordstrom, Urban Outfitters, Tilly's, Dick's Sporting Goods, Sportsman's Warehouse, Walgreens.
**More named:** Sam's Club, Belk, Beall's, Home Depot, Lowe's, Frontier Co-op, EE Distribution, gap.com, nautica.com.
**Tax-exempt friendly** (accept a reseller certificate for no-sales-tax B2B buys): **Home Depot, Lowe's, Walmart**.

**Note:** the creators say the best margins come from **"lowkey" lesser-known sites**, not Walmart/Target (too many sellers watching). **Never source from eBay or AliExpress** (counterfeit risk).

- **Retail trackers:** BrickSeek (Walmart/Target stock + clearance), Slickdeals (community deals), ProfitPath Dealwatch (price-drop alerts).

## Outsourcing (later, not as a beginner)
- **Prep center / 3PL** (~$1–2/unit): receives your buys, preps + forwards to FBA; some in tax-free states save sales tax.
- **Virtual assistants** ($3–5/hr, often Philippines): sourcing + order tracking. Share a VA with a trusted partner to split cost.
- **Business credit** for float (AMEX Plum ~60-day terms; 0% APR intro cards ~12-mo float) — only after you understand cash flow; start with $1–3k of your own money.
